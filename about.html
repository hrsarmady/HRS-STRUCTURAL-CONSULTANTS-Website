About Us

Experience that informs every decision.

HRS Structural Consultants brings technical depth, practical judgment, and direct collaboration to structural engineering challenges across building types.

Qualifications

Structural expertise across materials, markets, and project phases.

The firm’s qualifications include structural analysis, design documentation, peer review, assessment of existing structures, tenant improvement engineering, seismic and lateral-system design, and construction-phase support.

Experience includes wood, concrete, steel, masonry, podium construction, concrete towers, tilt-up structures, laboratories, civic facilities, hospitality, commercial offices, residential buildings, and industrial improvements.

Our approach is practical: clear communication, coordinated drawings, buildable details, and responsive support from early design through construction.
